Rules and Regulations

Welcome to the Cyber Conference! To ensure a safe, respectful, and productive environment for all participants, please read and adhere to the following rules and regulations:

Code of Conduct

The Cyber Conference is committed to providing a harassment-free experience for all attendees, regardless of gender, age, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, religion, or disability. All participants are expected to follow the conference's Code of Conduct:

  • Be respectful and considerate towards fellow attendees, speakers, and organizers.
  • Refrain from any form of harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior.
  • Do not engage in disruptive activities during conference sessions.
  • Obey all local, national, and international laws and regulations.
  • Any violation of the Code of Conduct may result in immediate removal from the conference without refund.

Recording and Photography

Unauthorized audio or video recording of conference sessions, workshops, or presentations is strictly prohibited. Photography is allowed unless specified otherwise by the speaker or event organizers.

Data Privacy and Security

As this is a cyber conference, we prioritize data privacy and security. All attendees are responsible for keeping their login credentials and personal information secure. Do not share your account details with others.

Software and Tools Usage

Usage of any unauthorized software or tools that may cause harm, disruption, or violation of the conference's security protocols is strictly forbidden.
