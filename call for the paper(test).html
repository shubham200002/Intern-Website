Call for Papers

We invite researchers, professionals, and enthusiasts to submit their original and unpublished research papers for the upcoming conference on Cyber Security. The conference aims to bring together experts from various domains to share their knowledge and insights on cybersecurity-related topics. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Network Security
  • Web Application Security
  • Data Privacy
  • IoT Security
  • Cloud Security
  • Malware Analysis
  • Threat Intelligence
  • Web Exclation
  • Android security
  • vulnerabilities Detections
  • OWASP
  • Kali Linux
  • SEO
  • Blue and Red teams

Submissions will be peer-reviewed, and accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings. We look forward to your valuable contributions to make this conference a success.

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: 15th September 2023
  • Notification of Acceptance: 30th October 2023
  • Camera-Ready Paper Due: 15th November 2023
  • Conference Dates: 10th - 12th January 2024

Submission Guidelines

Submissions should be in English and adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Papers must be original and not previously published or under review elsewhere.
  • Manuscripts should be formatted as per the IEEE conference template.
  • Submissions should be in PDF format and submitted through the conference website.
  • Each paper should include an abstract of no more than 250 words.
  • Authors are allowed to submit a maximum of two papers.
